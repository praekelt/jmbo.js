Jmbo apps


Introduction

Jmbo apps doesn't want to be a framework. However I don't know how one goes about providing a collection of "things" that formalizes a method of building HTML5 apps without becoming a framework.

So whilst one of my goals is not to be a framework I think it's inevitable. That said: We should try and be clear and simple in our implementations and minimal in css declarations.

But not too simple and not too minimal. It's meant to increase productivity afterall. :P

Requirements

I hope we don't ever require more than this:

  1. Underscore
  2. Backbone
  3. Zepto or jQuery

Getting started...

Apps don't generally refresh when a new page is requested/ loaded., rather new pages (or views) are loaded when required and transitioned into the viewport.

Stack Controller View

Is a drill down hierarchal stack.

You can keep pushing views onto the controller's stack. (Click "Run..." multiple times!)

Or you can remove them.

Tab Bar Controller View

Allows the app to switch contexts. Usually to navigation controller, or a single page.
